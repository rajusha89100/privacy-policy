TimeKeeper — Privacy Policy

1. About TimeKeeper

TimeKeeper is a lightweight, local-only time and task tracking app designed to help you log sessions, set simple timers, and view your local history. The app runs completely on your device: it does not require an account, does not access the internet, and does not ask for any device permissions.

2. Data we collect

Short answer: None by default.

  • TimeKeeper stores all user data locally on your device only (for example: timers, session logs, local preferences).
  • No personal information (name, email, contacts) is collected by the app.
  • No analytics, advertising identifiers, or crash reports are collected or transmitted.
Because the app is offline and permission-free, no data is sent off your device by the app itself.

3. Permissions & network

TimeKeeper does not request or require any permissions (camera, contacts, location, storage, etc.). The app is fully functional without any permission prompts.

TimeKeeper does not use the internet. The app contains no code that uploads data to servers, uses cloud APIs, or sends telemetry.

4. Local storage & backups

Your app data is kept locally on the device. If you use your device's backup or cloud backup features (for example: iCloud Backup, Google Drive backup on Android), those services may include the app data in device backups according to the device's settings. TimeKeeper itself does not initiate cloud backups.

If you wish to prevent remote backups of this app's local data, please review and disable relevant device backup settings.

5. Third-party services

TimeKeeper does not include third-party SDKs, advertising networks, or analytics libraries. There are no in-app trackers or external dependencies that collect user data.

6. Children

TimeKeeper is not intended specifically for children. Since the app collects no personal data and operates locally, parental controls are handled at the device level.

7. Data retention & deletion

All data is retained on the device until you delete the app or clear the app data through your device settings. Uninstalling TimeKeeper will remove the app and its local data in accordance with your device's uninstall behavior.

If your device performs automatic backups, copies of the app data might remain in those backups — consult your device or backup provider to remove backups if you want complete removal.

8. Security

Because data remains on your device, standard device-level security applies (device passcode, biometric lock, disk encryption). TimeKeeper does not add remote access to your data.

While we take care to avoid common security pitfalls in the app code, no software can be guaranteed bug-free. If you discover a security issue, please contact us (see below).

9. Changes to this privacy policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes that affect how data is handled, the updated policy will include the new effective date. Since the app is offline and permission-free, changes are normally informational (clarifications, wording) rather than functional.
